Darood Sharif:

Allahumma salli 'ala Muhammadin wa 'ala aali Muhammadin, kamaa sallaita 'ala Ibrahima wa 'ala aali Ibrahima innaka Hameedum Majeed.

Allahumma barik 'ala Muhammadin wa 'ala aali Muhammadin, kamaa barakta 'ala Ibrahima wa 'ala aali Ibrahima innaka Hameedum Majeed.

Rabbana Atina Dua:

Rabbana atina fi-dunya hasanatan wa fi-l-akhirati hasanatan wa qina 'adhaban-nar.

Anuvaad (Hindi): "He hamaare Palanhar! Hamen duniya mein bhalai de aur aakhirat mein bhi bhalai de, aur hamen aag ke azaab se bacha."

Dil Se Maangi Gayi Ek Behtareen Dua:

Ya Allah! Main kamzor hoon, be-bas hoon, lekin tera banda hoon. Har jagah dhakke khata hoon, har raste pe girta hoon, lekin jab tujhe pukarta hoon, to sukoon milta hai. Duniya ke saare darwaze band ho sakte hain, magar tera dar kabhi band nahi hota.

Ya Rahman! Jo zakhm sirf tu dekh sakta hai, unko bhar de. Jo dukh sirf tu samajh sakta hai, unko door kar de. Jo gham meri ruh ko tod raha hai, usko apni rehmat se mita de. Jo log mujhe samajh nahi paaye, unke dil narm kar de. Aur jo log mere apne hote hue bhi mujh se door ho gaye, unko wapas le aa.

Ya Raheem! Mujhe itni taqat de ke main kisi ka dil na dukhaun. Itni hikmat de ke sahi faisle kar sakun. Itni sabr de ke har mushkil ko tere hawaale kar du. Itni shukr guzaar zubaan de ke har haalat mein 'Alhamdulillah' keh sakun.

Ya Ghaffar! Jo gunah jaane anjaane mujh se ho gaye, unko maaf kar de. Jo cheez meri takdeer mein likhi hai, usko behtareen bana de. Jo manzil mere haq mein behtar hai, us tak pahunchne ka rasta dikha de. Aur jo cheez mere naseeb mein nahi hai, uske liye mere dil se tamanna nikal de.

Ya Malik! Agar meri duaein tere hikmat ke mutabiq behtar nahi hain, to mujhe wo de jo mere liye behtareen ho. Agar meri tamanna mere liye fitna ban sakti hai, to usse mere dil se nikal de. Agar main kisi raste par gumrah ho raha hoon, to mujhe waapas apni roshni ki taraf le aa.

Ya Wadood! Jo log mujh se mohabbat karte hain, unko mere haq mein behtar bana. Jo rishtay toot rahe hain, unko jod de. Jo dukh meri zindagi mein barh rahe hain, unko sukoon mein badal de. Aur jo bhi mere dil ki asli halat ko jaanta hai, bas tu hai, to mere dukh door kar de.

Ya Noor! Andheron se nikalne ka rasta dikha de. Tera ek noor meri zindagi ko roshan kar sakta hai. Tera ek karam meri sari pareshaniyan khatam kar sakta hai. Tera ek "Kun" kehna meri sari mushkilon ka hal la sakta hai.

Ya Allah! Jab tak zinda rahoon, tera shukar ada karta rahoon. Tera zikr karta rahoon. Tere hukm par chalta rahoon. Aur jab meri zindagi ka akhri din aaye, to meri ruh ko is haal mein le jaana ke tu mujh se raazi ho, aur main tujh se.

Ameen Ya Rabbal Aalameen.
